# Known Regression Classes

Use these as regression fixtures, not as one-off manual repair instructions.

- Blank or title-only cover passed as final.
- Retry/chunk merge appended duplicate story pages.
- Main character changed face, body, costume, props, or rendering style across pages.
- Supporting character was replaced by the wrong species or a duplicate protagonist.
- Raster illustrations contained gibberish, signatures, URLs, labels, or watermarks.
- Page illustration did not depict the story text.
- Story chronology was assembled out of order.
- Raw Markdown or HTML comments appeared in the PDF or public preview.
- Internal story brief appeared as customer-facing product description.
- Sale-page page count/read time differed from the final PDF.
- Technical asset access failure was recorded as quality score zero.
- Recovery worker repeated identical repairs without output or score improvement.
