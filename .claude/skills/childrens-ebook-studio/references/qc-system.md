# Quality Control System

## Hard release gates

Any failure blocks `production_ready`:
- age-inappropriate sexual, graphic, hateful, exploitative, or instructional dangerous content
- glamorized self-harm, abuse, substance use, or reckless imitation risk
- unresolved severe fear for young children
- copied or highly derivative protected characters, plot sequence, title, or artwork
- continuity contradiction that changes story meaning
- missing ending or unearned resolution
- unreadable cover title zone or misleading cover promise
- illustration prompt requests embedded body text
- incomplete pages, duplicate pages, or missing front matter
- factual/educational claim presented as true but unverified

## Weighted scorecard (100)

### Story and child experience — 35
- hook and clarity: 5
- protagonist agency: 5
- escalation and pacing: 5
- emotional arc: 5
- satisfying ending: 5
- read-aloud quality: 5
- reread value: 5

### Age and safety — 20
- vocabulary fit: 5
- sentence/plot complexity fit: 5
- emotional intensity fit: 5
- child safety and positive framing: 5

### Visual production — 20
- visual bible completeness: 4
- character consistency: 5
- page composition variety: 4
- text-safe layout: 3
- cover thumbnail strength: 4

### Originality and commercial readiness — 15
- concept originality: 5
- title/positioning: 3
- series potential: 2
- metadata completeness: 2
- market differentiation: 3

### Technical and editorial — 10
- grammar and spelling: 3
- continuity: 3
- page/word count compliance: 2
- production specifications: 2

## Thresholds

- 92-100: production_ready, provided all hard gates pass.
- 85-91: editorial_review.
- 70-84: needs_revision.
- below 70: draft.

No category may score below 70% of its available points for production-ready status.

## Three-pass auto-revision

### Pass 1: structural
Repair plot logic, agency, pacing, climax, ending, page turns, and age fit.

### Pass 2: language and visual continuity
Repair rhythm, vocabulary, repetition, grammar, visual ledger, character details, and text density.

### Pass 3: commercial and production
Repair title, cover concept, metadata, thumbnail clarity, page completeness, and trim/safe-zone notes.

After each pass, rescore only after rereading the complete book as a whole.

## Safety review prompts

Check:
- Could a child imitate a dangerous action?
- Is an adult or trusted helper needed but absent?
- Does the ending normalize bullying, secrecy, coercion, or humiliation?
- Are fears resolved appropriately for the age?
- Are cultural, disability, family, and gender depictions respectful and non-tokenizing?
- Does educational content require fact verification?

## Cover QC

At 10% thumbnail size:
- Can the main character be identified?
- Is the emotional tone obvious?
- Is there a clean title area?
- Is the silhouette distinctive?
- Does the cover match the actual story?
- Is it visually distinct from known franchises?

## Human review notice

For commercial release, require final human proofreading, rights review, print proof, and platform-specific compliance check. AI QC reduces errors but is not a legal or publishing guarantee.
